<p><a class="accordion-trigger ques" id="ques27"><strong>Question 28. Write Short Notes On Neighbours Of A Pixel?</strong></a></p>
<p><span class="ans"><strong>Answer :</strong></span><ul>
<li>The pixel p at co-ordinates (x, y) has 4 neighbors (ie) 2 horizontal and 2 vertical neighbors whose co-ordinates is given by (x+1, y), (x-1,y), (x,y-1), (x, y+1). This is called as direct neighbors. It is denoted by N4(P)</li>
<li>Four diagonal neighbors of p have co-ordinates (x+1, y+1), (x+1,y-1), (x-1, y-1),(x-1, y+1). It is denoted by ND(4).</li>
<li>Eight neighbors of p denoted by N8(P) is a combination of 4 direct neighbors and 4 diagonal neighbors.</li>
</ul></p>
